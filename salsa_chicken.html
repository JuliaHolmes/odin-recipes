One Pan Salsa Chicken

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 1 bag frozen corn (3 cups)
  • 1 can drained & rinsed black beans
  • 1 can drained petite diced tomatoes (don't rinse)
  • 1 cup favorite salsa
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt, pepper, pepper flakes, chili powder to taste

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375 degrees farenheit
  2. Stir together corn, beans, tomatoes, 1/2 cup salsa, garlic and all spices in deep glass cooking/baking pan.
  3. Place chicken breast on top, salt & pepper both sides
  4. Pour rest of salsa on top of chicken breast
  5. Bake for 45-60 minutes until chicken hits 165 degrees internal temperature
  6. Remove chicken from dish and shred, chunk, or slice.Return to dish and mix
  7. Serve with rice or tortillas and enjoy!
